Welcome to Sunrise Consulting (UK) Ltd

ABOUT US

Sunrise Consulting (UK) Ltd is run by the husband-and-wife team of Peter and Judith Ward. Peter specialises in life coaching and personal development. Judith is a Landscape Architect. Both are committed Christians and bring the Christian ethos to their work.
Peter is in the process of updating this website to reflect his focus on adherence. This is as a result of 4 years of PhD research into adherence, and especially medicine adherence -- whether people take their medicine when they should -- at the University of Warwick. For now, please follow the link to adherence to learn more about his work and his consulting offer.
Judith has worked as a Landscape Architect for over 20 years. Her experience spans everything from private gardens to major inner-city regeneration projects. Click here for more details on Judith's background. She offers a range of Landscape-related services from bespoke design projects through onsite management to complete one-stop-shop solutions.
